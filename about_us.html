SHORT TAIL VETERINARY SERVICES

At Short Tail Veterinary Services, we are dedicated to providing compassionate and affordable veterinary services. Located at General Tinio St., Cabanatuan City (beside Yogi Café), we offer clinic visits, home services, low-cost kapon (spay and neuter), and online consultations. As the first low-cost kapon clinic in Cabanatuan City, we strive to promote responsible pet care, ensuring that #EveryTailMatters.
